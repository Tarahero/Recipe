veggiegarlicnoodle

Steps for making veggie garlic noodle

  1. Cook the Noodles:
    • Boil a large pot of water and cook the noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente.
    • Drain the cooked noodles and set them aside.


  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and sugar (if using). Set the sauce aside.


  3. Sauté the Vegetables:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat
    • Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
    • Add the sliced mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender but still crisp.


  4. Combine Everything
    • Add the cooked noodles to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables
    • Pour the sauce over the noodles and vegetables.
    • Toss everything together gently to ensure the noodles are well
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the sauce, sugar, and spice levels according to your preferences.
