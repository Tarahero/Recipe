Steps for making momo

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt.
    • Gradually add water while kneading until you have a smooth, pliable dough.
    • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.


  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • Heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    • Add chopped onions and green chilies, and sauté until the onions become translucent.
    • Stir in the finely chopped vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes until they soften slightly.
    • Add soy sauce, sesame oil (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
    • Remove the filling from the heat and let it cool.


  3. Assemble the Momos:
    • Take a small portion of the rested dough and roll it into a thin circle (about 3-4 inches in diameter) using a rolling pin.
    • Place a spoonful of the cooled vegetable filling in the center of the dough circle.


  4. Fold and Seal the Momos:
    • Take a small portion of the rested dough and roll it into a thin circle (about 3-4 inches in diameter) using a rolling pin.
    • Place a spoonful of the cooled vegetable filling in the center of the dough circle.


  5. Fold and seal the momos
    • Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape.
    • Pinch and pleat the edges of the dough to seal the momo. You can use various folding techniques for different shapes

    • Place the prepared momos on a greased steamer tray or steamer basket, ensuring they don't touch each other.
    • Steam the momos for 15-20 minutes, or until the dough becomes translucent and the filling is cooked through.
