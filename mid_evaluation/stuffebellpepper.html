stuffed bell pepper

Steps for making stuffed bell papper

  1. Prepare the bellpeppers:
    • Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse them.


  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a skillet, cook the ground meat, chopped onions, and minced garlic until the meat is browned and the onions are translucent.
    • Drain any excess fat.


  3. Mix the Ingredients:
    • In a bowl, combine the cooked meat mixture with cooked rice and tomato sauce or diced tomatoes.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and your choice of herbs and spices.


  4. Stuff the Bell Peppers:
    • Spoon the filling mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers.


  5. Bake
    • Place the stuffed bell peppers in a baking dish.
    • Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
    6. ]

  6. Optional Cheese Topping:
    • If desired, remove the foil, sprinkle grated cheese on top of each stuffed pepper, and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
